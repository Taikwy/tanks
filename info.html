TANKS! is a solo project I developed in 2021. Originally a college assignment, I thought the game's concept to be interesting enough to spend more time on it. I decided to almost entirely remake the game from the ground up, an endeavor which took around 8 months.


I programmed TANKS! entirely with JavaScript, using the PixiJS library to handle the graphical elements and HowlerJS for the audio. All of the visual elements (user interface, sprites, animations, and backgrounds) were drawn from scratch by me with Aseprite. Sound effects and music were taken from free audio packs I found on Itch.io, with their links listed in the CREDITS section.
